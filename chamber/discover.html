Discover Rajahmundry

One of the most famous temples in Andhra Pradesh, Iskcon Temple is a recreational centre in Rajahmundry. It is known as the second-largest Iskcon Temple in the Southern region of India after the Iskcon in Bangalore. Also known as Gowthami Ghat, this temple is a popular worship spot and is definitely worth a visit. Built across 2 acres of land on the banks of the Godavari River, this temple was created by the Iskcon group which stands for International Society for Krishna Consciousness and is a temple primarily dedicated to Lord Krishna. Many Rishis are believed to have performed Tapasya or prayer here and has a few alternative names such as Sri Gopinath Temple and Ramananda Raya Gaudiya Math since it is believed that Sri Chaitanya and Ramananda Raya exchanged words on this site. It is one of the most modern temples with a number of facilities available for tourists and should be on the list for those who wish to see Hindu culture in South India.

The eastern bank of Godavari river has many boating services where they will take you out for a ride on the water and take you to the small islands in the middle of the river and bring you back for very cheap price.They live by finishing and giving boat rides and providing laundy serives.

The highlight of the city is the Dowleswaram Barrage which is built across River Godavari and is one of the top tourist attractions in the region. The dam was built by Sir Arthur Cotton. In the memory of the aforementioned personality, a museum has also been constructed in town which displays projects and demos relating to the irrigation works.

The beauty of nature is found in the forest of Maredumilli this location has lots of good view points the sunrise from the top of mountains around 5 or 6 AM in the morning looks unreal and so beautiful making the place good for friends to hang out for the night and witness the sunrise.

Sir Arthur Cotton Museum was established in the fond memory of the great engineer Sir Arthur Cotton. The museum exhibits various models which depict the construction of the mighty barrage across Godavari River by this British irrigation engineer. He was also known as Cotton Dora and had earned popularity for his efforts for making Godavari River a fertile water resource. He was also the Delta Architect of Godavari District. He is still remembered for his excellent irrigation engineering works among which the prominent one includes the Dowleswaram Barrage and Anicut System.

Located in the heart of the city this is the best 3 Star Hotel in the city and the food is great and it has a swimming pool on the roof and affordable pricing for the stay with good packages for long vacations.

The view from the west bank of river Godavari is quite peaceful because of the less noise and less water pollution making the place very calming to sit down and enjoy.

Shivalingam Statue was very famous attraction in the city for the follower of God Shiva and the lights light up every night and the water fountain never stops flowing and lot of devotional activites take palce often.

Papikondalu is the tribal area with the beautifully flowing river Godavari flowing in between the mountains. The tourism has several packages to stay in the sand in the middle of water for a couple of day and a boat ride.
